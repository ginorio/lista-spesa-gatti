Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 21, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Lista Spesa Famiglia. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle your personal data when you use our application.

2. Data We Collect

We collect and process the following data:

  • Account Information: Email address, display name (optional)
  • Shopping List Data: Product names, quantities, custom names, comments, and locations you add to your shopping lists
  • Authentication Data: If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account email and profile information

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain the shopping list service
  • Authenticate your account and ensure security
  • Sync your shopping lists across devices
  • Improve our services based on usage patterns

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Lovable Cloud (powered by Supabase). We implement:

  • Row-Level Security (RLS) to ensure users can only access their own data
  • Encrypted connections for all data transmission
  • Secure authentication protocols

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • When you explicitly share your shopping list via WhatsApp or PDF export
  • As required by law or to protect our rights
  • With our trusted service providers (Lovable Cloud/Supabase) who help us operate the application

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: For authentication (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Lovable Cloud/Supabase: For data storage and backend services
  • Google Gemini API: For OCR functionality (when you import from photos)

8. Cookies and Local Storage

We use local storage and session storage in your browser to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Store temporary data for better performance

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for family use. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@lista-spesa-famiglia.app

12. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union, we comply with GDPR requirements. Your data is processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently. You have full rights over your data as described in Section 6.
